Banquo – Key Characteristics in Macbeth

Clear, concise notes you can revise quickly.

Loyal

Remains faithful to King Duncan and does not join any plot against him.

Honest & Noble

Avoids evil actions; refuses to act immorally to fulfil the prophecy.

Wise & Cautious

Distrusts the witches and warns that they may be agents of harm.

Brave

A respected soldier who fights courageously in battle.

Moral Integrity

Resists temptation and does not let ambition control his actions.

Foil & Victim

His goodness highlights Macbeth’s corruption; Macbeth has him murdered out of fear of the prophecy.
