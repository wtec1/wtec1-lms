Applications of Density

Relative Density

Definition:

The ratio of the mass of a substance to the mass of an equal volume of water

RD = Mass of substance / Mass of equal volume of water

Important: Relative Density has NO UNITS

Alternative Definition:

RD can also be expressed as:

RD = Density of substance / Density of water

Example Calculation:

Given:

  • ‚Ä¢ Empty bottle mass = 20 g
  • ‚Ä¢ Bottle + water = 40 g
  • ‚Ä¢ Bottle + liquid X = 30 g

Calculations:

Mass of water = 40 - 20 = 20 g

Mass of liquid X = 30 - 20 = 10 g

Answer:

RD = 10/20 = 0.5

Liquid X is half as dense as water
