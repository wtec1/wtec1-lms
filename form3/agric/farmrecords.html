Purpose of Financial Records

Financial records provide a complete picture of your farm's economic performance. They show cash flow, help calculate profit or loss, and are essential for determining the overall financial health of your farming business.

By maintaining accurate financial records, you can identify profitable enterprises, make informed decisions about resource allocation, and ensure your farm remains economically viable for the long term.
